## 2. Conversion + economics benchmarks (the numbers that govern our funnel)

- **Wordfence: 130k paying / 5M+ installs = 2.6% conversion** (self-disclosed 2024) — the cleanest security-category benchmark.
- **Freemius averages: 3–4% free→paid WITH active upgrade architecture; sub-1% passive.** Opt-in trial 18%, card-required 50%.
- **Rank Math ratio: ~$0.73 revenue per free install per year** — WP audience price sensitivity.
- Revenue distribution: 70% of Freemius makers < $1k MRR; median profitable ~$4.2k MRR; top 1% > $50k MRR.
- CodeCanyon: avg author $3,838 gross/yr; 61.6% of plugins sell <100 licenses; 0.5% sell >10k. Avg price $18.91.
- Standalone plugin price ceiling ≈ **$200–300/yr/site**; above that only via agency/multi-site licensing.
- Plugin layer captures **<0.4%** of the $596B WP economy — license tolls are on-ramps; flows are the business.

**Our funnel math at benchmarks:** 100k installs × 2.6–4% × $59–99 ≈ **$150–400k ARR** from the toll alone.
Marketplace/flows must carry the rest — as designed (STRATEGY §6).

## 4. Structural reads

1. **Winner-take-most:** Elementor out-earns the next ~8 companies combined. Second tier $15–47M (forms/SEO/builders). Long tail is a graveyard.
2. **Pricing power = workflow-lock.** Daily-workflow products (builders, forms, SEO) hold price; utilities commoditize. → MCPWP must be the daily operator surface (Control Room/approvals/signals), not a configured-once connector.
3. **AI category is pre-revenue scale:** AI Engine 100k installs, GetGenie 30k, 10Web $5M ARR only disclosed. No incumbent. CodeWP absorbed by Automattic. **Window open.**
4. **Bootstrapped giants:** Elementor + Awesome Motive prove $50M ARR WP companies need no VC — lean + portfolio + active monetization architecture.
5. **Agency tooling is undisclosed + neglected** (ManageWP/GoDaddy stagnant; WP Umbrella winning migrations at €1.99/site/mo). Our Agency proxy at $4/site/mo competes in-band with an AI story none have.
6. **Distribution channels carry different DNA:** ThemeForest/CodeCanyon = one-time, huge units (Avada 1M sales); own-site = SaaS-stable (Yoast, WP Rocket). Our dual annual/lifetime offer matches both psychologies.
